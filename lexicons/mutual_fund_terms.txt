Here's a rundown of KTAM's KT-branded funds (KTAM = Krung Thai Asset Management, Krungthai's fund arm). I've grouped them and noted the underlying/master fund where it's known. Quick legend on the suffixes first, since they matter for your test cases:
Class suffixes: -A = สะสมมูลค่า (accumulation, no dividend) · -D = จ่ายปันผล (dividend) · -R = รับซื้อคืนอัตโนมัติ (auto-redemption) · -P = a pricing/channel class · H = FX-hedged · UI = ห้ามขายผู้ลงทุนรายย่อย (not for retail) · RMF / SSF / ThaiESG = tax-deductible wrappers of the same strategy.
